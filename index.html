About the Fund

The Central Valley Physician Education and Wellness Fund is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to supporting Emergency Medicine residents in their pursuit of excellence. We believe that a well-educated and healthy resident workforce strengthens our community and improves patient care.

Our residents are not just training to be outstanding physicians—they are actively engaged in serving and caring for our community. This fund ensures they have the resources and support necessary to grow professionally while maintaining their well-being.

🎓 Educational Support

Scholarships for conferences, courses, and continuing education

💚 Wellness Initiatives

Programs focused on mental health, burnout prevention, and self-care

🤝 Community-Centered

Supporting residents who give back to their communities

What We Fund

We invest in areas that create lasting impact on residents' professional and personal development

📚

Educational Conferences

Sponsorships and scholarships to attend national and regional emergency medicine conferences, enabling residents to stay current with the latest clinical advances.

🧠

Professional Development

Support for specialized training, certifications, research opportunities, and skill-building courses that advance resident expertise.

🧘

Wellness Programs

Funding for mental health resources, stress management workshops, fitness initiatives, and retreats that promote overall well-being.

💡

Innovation & Research

Support for resident-led research projects and quality improvement initiatives that contribute to the field of emergency medicine.

🤲

Community Outreach

Enabling residents to participate in community health initiatives and volunteer programs that serve vulnerable populations.

🎯

Resident Enrichment

Special events and experiences that foster camaraderie, leadership development, and work-life balance among residents.

Our Mission

"To empower Emergency Medicine residents by providing financial support for educational advancement and wellness initiatives, fostering a community of compassionate, well-rounded physicians who excel in clinical practice and serve their communities with dedication and excellence."
